# Migonest App Marketing Assets

This directory contains the visual assets and marketing copy for the Migonest App campaign.

## Visual Assets

### 1. Final Marketing Banner
- **File**: `migonest_final_banner.png`
- **Description**: Diverse group of students and experts with the Migonest "M" logo and unified headline.
- **Headline**: "Migonest: Where Aspirations Meet Experience"
- **Sub-headline**: "Connecting students with global experts."

### 2. Base Group Image
- **File**: `migonest_base_banner.png`
- **Description**: High-resolution photography of diverse international students (including Hijabi, non-Hijabi, and male students) in a modern campus setting. Use this as a base for custom social media posts.

## Marketing Copy & Campaign Options

### Option 1: Unified Community (Recommended)
> **Headline**: "Migonest: Where Aspirations Meet Experience"
> **Body**: The ultimate bridge between prospective students and university experts already living the dream abroad. Inclusive, Professional, Global.

### Option 2: For Prospective Students
> **Headline**: "From Dreams to Destinations."
> **Body**: Your journey abroad starts with a mentor who’s been there. Connect with experts, secure your admission, and start your global story with Migonest.

### Option 3: For Experts Abroad

> **Body**: You’ve made it abroad—now help others do the same. Join the Migonest Expert community, share your journey, and empower the next generation of global students.

## Brand Guidelines
- **Logo**: The blue "M" square icon should be placed in the top-left corner (Power Corner) for maximum recognition.
- **Font**: Use bold, clean sans-serif typography (e.g., Inter, Montserrat, or Roboto).
- **Vibe**: Inspiring, Professional, and Multicultural.
